About RecipeAI

RecipeAI helps users turn available ingredients into practical recipe ideas with AI-powered image understanding and smart text matching.

Our Goal

Make home cooking easier by recommending recipes from ingredients users already have.

How It Works

The app combines image detection, OCR extraction, and ingredient-based ranking to suggest relevant recipes.

Who It Helps

Students, families, and beginner cooks who want quick meal ideas without wasting ingredients.

Technology Stack

Backend

Python, Flask, REST APIs

AI / ML

TensorFlow/Keras, image inference, confidence filtering

Data

Processed recipe dataset + optional USDA/OpenFoodFacts raw sources

Frontend

Jinja templates, Tailwind utility classes, vanilla JavaScript
